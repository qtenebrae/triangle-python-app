1.  Enter the coordinates of the vertices of the triangle in the corresponding fields on the screen.
2.  Enter the coordinates of the point from which the ball will be released into the corresponding fields on the screen.
3.  Enter the initial velocity of the ball into the appropriate fields on the screen.
4.  Press the Start button to start the ball moving.
5.  The ball will move inside the triangular area, bouncing off the walls.
6.  Press the "Stop" button to stop the ball from moving.
7.  To reset the values of the variables to the original values, press the "Values by mind" button.
